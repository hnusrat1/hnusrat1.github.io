Bridging Physics & Patient Care

I'm a medical physicist dedicated to advancing cancer treatment through cutting-edge technology. Currently serving as Senior Staff Physicist at Henry Ford Cancer in Detroit, I specialize in MR-guided adaptive radiotherapy and stereotactic radiosurgery.

My passion lies in pushing the boundaries of what's possible, from developing virtual reality training simulators to creating AI-powered tools for autonomous treatment planning. I believe in making complex physics accessible and using innovation to improve patient outcomes.
